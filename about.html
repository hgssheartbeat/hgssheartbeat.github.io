Hillgrove Heartbeat started in 2014 & we are the Official Radio Station of Hillgrove Secondary School.

 

We are keep students up-to-date with Global & Local News by having a Morning News Session before our Morning Assembly.

 

We are also in charge of being the Music Entertainment during School Events, such as Speech Day.

 

We train students to be more confident & to be able to gain the experience of being a DJ.

 

Our Teacher-in-charge is Mr. Ashley Hsu.
